Hey There!

My name is Fonix Hardcastle. I'm a British Network Engineer & Web Developer in his Teens, currently working freelance.
I love all things technical, loud ambient house music, milk and the smell of BBQ chicken.

I started doing what I do by now about ten years ago and although I took a more audio-oriented detour in between — well, not really a detour, just a different approach to fulfill my need for creativity — I eventually came back to doing what I used to do about two years ago. I've worked very hard ever since trying to improve my skills in Network Engineering & Web Development. My goal was not only to be able to offer my clients a broad spectrum of services, but to really accompany them from start to finish of a project. That's why — unlike many others in the industry — I decided to not specialize in either networking or development, but to do both. Also: It wouldn't have made sense to choose between the two, if I'm actually passionate about both fields, right?

Over the course of the last two years, I've had the chance to work with multiple clients and agencies of all sorts and was able to gain a lot of experience. Experience that helped me improving my skills in communication, project management and strengthen my knowledge of the industry in general.
